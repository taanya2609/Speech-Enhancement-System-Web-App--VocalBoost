About VocalBoost

Our mission is to make clear audio accessible to everyone.

What is VocalBoost?

VocalBoost is an AI-powered speech enhancement system designed to transform noisy and distorted audio recordings into clear, intelligible speech. We leverage a deep learning model to accurately identify and remove unwanted background noise, ensuring your message comes through loud and clear.

The Technology Behind It

At its core, VocalBoost uses a Denoising Autoencoder, a type of neural network specifically trained on a massive dataset of paired noisy and clean speech. The model learns to reconstruct the clean audio signal from a noisy input by operating on its spectrogram representation. This approach allows for highly effective noise reduction without sacrificing the natural quality of the voice.

Our Vision

We believe that clear communication is a fundamental need. VocalBoost aims to provide an easy-to-use, accessible tool that helps podcasters, students, journalists, and anyone with a noisy recording to instantly improve their audio quality. Our goal is to empower users with professional-grade audio enhancement right from their web browser.
